Create a premium, modern, minimal, and professional IT Engineer Portfolio Website.

IMPORTANT DESIGN DIRECTION:
Do NOT make it look like a typical AI-themed portfolio full of glowing effects and futuristic elements. The design should feel elegant, clean, modern, and industry-level professional. Use AI and neural network visuals only as subtle background elements.

COLOR PALETTE:

* Primary Background: Deep Matte Black (#080808)
* Secondary Background: Dark Charcoal (#121212)
* Accent Colors:

  * Soft Purple (#B48EFF)
  * Soft Pink (#FFB6E6)
  * White (#FFFFFF)
  * Light Lavender (#E6D9FF)
* Use gradients carefully and minimally.
* Avoid neon cyberpunk colors.

TYPOGRAPHY:

* Use modern premium fonts.
* Headings: Space Grotesk / Satoshi / Clash Display
* Body: Inter / Manrope
* Smooth font rendering.
* Professional spacing and hierarchy.

GLOBAL BACKGROUND:
Across the entire portfolio, add a very subtle 3D neural network structure floating in the background.
Requirements:

* Low opacity (5-10%)
* Slight movement
* Soft blur effect
* Connected nodes and lines
* Looks premium and scientific
* Should not distract from content
* Present across all sections
